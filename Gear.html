Chapter 5

Home Gym Equipment

🏋️
Adjustable Dumbbells
The only piece of equipment you truly need. I recommend sets with a quick-adjust dial mechanism — Bowflex 552 or Core Home Fitness are both solid. Start at 5 lbs, grow to 50.
🪑
Adjustable Bench
Flat to 45-degree incline. 17 inches high when flat. Solid locking mechanism. Narrow enough for free arm movement. You can find a good one for $100–200 — mine came secondhand.
🧘
Rubber Floor Mat
Protects your floor and stops equipment sliding. Not glamorous but essential if you're training on hardwood or tile. One 4x6 foot mat handles most setups.
Chapter 10

Supplements I Actually Take

🥛
Whey Protein
1 scoop post-workout. ~25g protein. Go for low-sugar. Fast-digesting — gets to muscle tissue when it counts most. Non-negotiable after 50 when protein synthesis slows down.
⚡
Creatine Monohydrate
5g daily. Mix into water or your shake. No loading phase needed. One of the most well-researched supplements in existence — safe, effective, and cheap. Don't overthink it.
☕
Collagen Peptides
1 scoop in morning coffee. Unflavored mixes invisibly. Supports joints, tendons, and connective tissue — the stuff that takes the longest to recover after 40.
🌙
Magnesium Glycinate
200–400mg before bed. Improves sleep quality and reduces post-workout cramping. Glycinate form is easiest on the stomach. Start low and adjust.
💊
Zinc
15–30mg daily with food. Supports immune function and helps maintain testosterone levels. Don't overdo it — more isn't better with zinc.
🧴
Electrolytes
LMNT or Nuun on training days. Especially important if you're in a warm climate or sweating heavily. Prevents fatigue and cramping without the sugar hit of sports drinks.
Chapter 11

Sleep & Recovery

😴
Sleep Mask / Blackout Curtains
Light disrupts melatonin even through closed eyelids. A quality sleep mask costs almost nothing and makes a measurable difference in sleep depth. One of the highest-ROI purchases on this list.
🧊
Cold Plunge / Cold Therapy
Reduces acute inflammation, speeds perceived recovery, and has a surprisingly strong effect on mood and mental clarity. Cold showers are free. Purpose-built cold plunges are the upgrade.
🌡️
Sauna Blanket
Heat promotes circulation, relaxes connective tissue, and has solid longevity research behind it. A sauna blanket is a fraction of the cost of a full sauna with most of the benefit.
🫧
Foam Roller / Massage Gun
Active recovery between sessions. Breaks up adhesions, improves blood flow, and reduces soreness. Five minutes after training or before bed makes the next session noticeably easier.
Chapter 13

Movement & Tracking

⌚
Fitness Tracker / Smartwatch
10,000 steps doesn't count itself. A wrist tracker keeps you honest about daily movement volume — the most underrated health variable after 50. Sleep tracking as a bonus.
🧘
Yoga Mat
For floor work, stretching, yoga, and Pilates sessions. Non-slip surface matters more than thickness. A 5mm mat handles everything in this book.
🎯
Resistance Bands
Useful for warm-ups, mobility work, and as a regression tool when you're building into heavier movements. Light, cheap, and travel-friendly.
